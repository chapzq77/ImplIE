Freebase entries obtained by getting the names, adjectival forms, and aliases.

enable1.txt is a list of scrabble words (i.e. no proper nouns).  Accessed from
www.puzzlers.org/pub/wordlists/enable1.txt.
first_names.txt is a list of first names from 1990 census data.  Accessed from
deron.meranda.us/data/census-derived-all-first.txt.
last_names.txt is a list of last names from 1990 census data.  Accessed from
http://www2.census.gov/topics/genealogy/1990surnames/dist.all.last.
